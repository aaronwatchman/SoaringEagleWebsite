Who are we and what we do

Soaring Eagle Financial Services is a medical billing company based out of Phoenix, Arizona. It was founded in 1990 by former CEO Michelle Spencer when she decided to branch out on her own. She organized a talented team and established a list of clients. Today, Soaring Eagle Financial Services serves medical providers throughout Native American reservations.

Our Goals

In the current climate, many medical providers have found it essential to have locally based medical billing company that can provide solutions and services with revenue in mind. With our accountability and experienced knowledge, we aim to help providers streamline the medical billing process.
